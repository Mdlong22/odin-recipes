Skillet Chicken Bulgogi

This is a really tasty recipe that I got from online

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 1/2 table spoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into thin strips

Steps

  1. Whisk onion, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, sesame oil, sesame seeds, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl until marinade is smooth.
  2. Cook and still chicken and marinade together in a large skillet over medium-high heat until chicken is cooked through, about 15 minutes
